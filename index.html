🚑 K9 EMERGENCY FIELD MEDICINE

1. K9 CPR: 100-120 bpm. Lay dog on RIGHT side. 30 compressions / 2 nose breaths.
2. GUNSHOT / STAB: Pack wound with gauze. Constant pressure. Do not remove blood-soaked pads.
3. HEAT EXHAUSTION: Temp >106°F is critical. Cool paws/ears with water. Move to AC immediately.
4. BLOAT (GDV): 10-43 Emergency. Hard/swollen stomach. Dry retching. Immediate surgery required.
5. SNAKE BITE: Keep dog calm/immobile. Transport to ER Vet. Do not cut or suck wound.
