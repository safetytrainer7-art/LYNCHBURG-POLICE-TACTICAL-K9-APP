🩹 K9 FIRST AID (CPR #1)
1. K9 CPR: 100-120 compressions/min. Right side down. Heart at left elbow. 30 pumps / 2 nose breaths.
2. GSW / STAB: Pack wound with gauze. Constant pressure. Do not remove soaked pads.
3. HEAT EXHAUSTION: Temp >106°F fatal. Cool paws/ears with water. Move to AC.
