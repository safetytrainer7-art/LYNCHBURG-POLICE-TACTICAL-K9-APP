🩹 EMERGENCY K9 FIRST AID

1. K9 CPR (PRIORITY ONE)

Check Breathing/Pulse: If no pulse, lay dog on RIGHT side on firm surface.

Compressions: Place hands over widest part of chest (behind elbow). Compress 1/3 to 1/2 depth of chest.

Rate: 100-120 per minute. 30 compressions then 2 quick breaths into the NOSE (hold mouth shut).

GUNSHOT/STAB: Pack wound with clean gauze. Apply heavy pressure. Do NOT remove soaked bandages; add more on top.
HEAT EXHAUSTION: Internal temp >106°F is critical. Apply tepid water to paws, ears, and belly. Move to AC. Do not use ice water (shocks system).
SNAKE BITE: Keep dog calm/immobilized. Note snake type if possible. Do NOT cut or suck wound. Transport to ER Vet immediately.
EYE INJURY: Do not allow dog to rub. Flush with saline only. Cover with a loose bandage or cone.
