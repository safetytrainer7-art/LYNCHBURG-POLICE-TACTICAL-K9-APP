#1 PRIORITY: K9 MEDICAL

K9 CPR

1. Lay dog on right side.
2. Compressions: 100-120 per min over heart (just behind elbow).
3. 30 compressions to 2 breaths (seal nose, blow into snout).
4. Check pulse every 2 mins.

K9 HEAT STROKE

Cool paws and ears with cool (not ice) water. Transport immediately.

K9 BLOAT (GDV)

Look for unproductive vomiting/swollen stomach. Time is life. Vet immediately.
