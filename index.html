K9 FIRST AID

K9 CPR

1. Lay dog on right side.
2. 100-120 compressions per minute.
3. 30 compressions to 2 breaths.

GUNSHOT / BLEED

1. Apply direct pressure.
2. Use hemostatic gauze if available.
3. Pack wound; do not remove soaked bandages.

SNAKE BITE

1. Keep K9 calm & immobilized.
2. Keep bite site below heart level.
3. Immediate transport to emergency vet.
