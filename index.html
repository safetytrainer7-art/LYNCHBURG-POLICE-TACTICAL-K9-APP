K9 FIELD TRAUMA SOS

IMMEDIATE ACTION PROTOCOLS

  • Heat Stress: Move to shade. Apply cool water to paws and groin. Monitor for rapid panting.
  • Trauma: Apply steady direct pressure to any active bleeding sites.
  • Bloat: Immediate transport required if the K9 is distended or dry heaving.
