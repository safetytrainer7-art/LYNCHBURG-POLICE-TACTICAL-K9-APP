1. K9 CPR PROTOCOL (IMMEDIATE)

• Lay dog on Right side. Find heart (left elbow).
• Compressions: 100-120 per minute.
• Mouth-to-Snout: 2 breaths for every 30 compressions.
• Maintain rhythm until K9 resuscitates or medical team takes over.
