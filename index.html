LEGAL SHIELD BIBLE

I. THE FOURTH AMENDMENT

The bedrock of patrol work. All searches must be 'reasonable' under the totality of circumstances.

II. PROBABLE CAUSE & 5 SENSES

PC exists when facts lead a prudent person to believe contraband is present. Use your sight, smell, and K9 alerts to build the case.

III. FEDERAL CASE LAW (TOP 10)

1. Rodriguez v. US: Do not prolong the stop for a K9 sniff.

2. Florida v. Jardines: No front porch sniffs without a warrant.
