K9 EMERGENCY FIELD MEDICINE

1. K9 CPR (PRIORITY): 100-120 bpm. RIGHT side down. 30 compressions then 2 nose breaths.
2. GUNSHOT / STAB: Pack wound with gauze. Constant pressure. Do not remove soaked pads.
3. HEAT EXHAUSTION: Temp >106°F is critical. Cool paws/ears with water. Move to AC.
4. SNAKE BITE: Keep dog calm. Immobilize. Transport to ER Vet. No cutting.
5. EXCESSIVE BLEEDING: Apply pressure points above wound. Tourniquet as last resort.
6. EYE INJURIES: Flush with saline only. Cover with cone/bandage.
