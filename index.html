1. Live Asset Synchronization

This "First in the Country" system provides a live, real-time visual of every asset in the search area. Law enforcement units (Blue), search and rescue (Red), and civilians (Green) are all tracked simultaneously to ensure total field awareness. By seeing every brother and sister on the screen, an officer can move with confidence, knowing exactly who is in the brush with them.

The Incident Commander has the authority to place or remove units from the map as the situation evolves. If a deputy moves to a new perimeter point, their dot follows them instantly. This real-time positioning is the ultimate tool for officer safety, eliminating the risk of accidental crossfire or units becoming lost in the deep terrain of the Blue Ridge.

2. Evidence & Property Flags

When an officer locates a scent article or piece of evidence—such as a jacket, hat, or weapon—they can instantly drop a Property Flag at their current GPS coordinate. This flag is visible to every unit on the search, allowing K9 handlers to vector their dogs directly to the item without breaking radio silence or contaminating the area.

These markers create a digital evidence trail that can be used later in court to prove the suspect's path of travel. By correlating property flags with the K9's track and GPS timestamps, the department provides an airtight, scientific account of the pursuit, from the first discovery to the final apprehension.

3. Scent Cone & Wind Integration

For the K9 handler, the wind is everything. This interface features a floating wind direction arrow that updates based on local weather data. By watching the red arrow, the handler can calculate the "Scent Cone" blowing off the suspect (Purple Dot). This allows the team to stay downwind, approaching with the element of surprise while maximizing the dog's ability to alert.

The system also calculates the live distance between searching officers. If units spread too far apart, a tactical alert sounds to close the gap. This ensures a thorough, 100% saturation search where nothing—and no one—can slip through the line unnoticed.
