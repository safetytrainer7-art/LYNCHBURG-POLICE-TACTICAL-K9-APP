GUNSHOT / PENETRATING TRAUMA

1. Pack wound with hemostatic gauze (QuikClot). 2. Apply pressure for 3-5 mins. 3. Apply chest seal for torso wounds. 4. Limb wounds: TQ high and tight.

SNAKEBITES (Copperhead/Timber Rattler)

1. Keep K9 calm and immobilized. 2. Keep bite site BELOW heart level. 3. Do NOT cut or use suction. 4. Transport to ER immediately for antivenin.

EYE INJURIES (Debris/Scratches)

1. Do NOT allow K9 to rub eye (use cone/collar). 2. Flush with saline ONLY. 3. Do not apply pressure to the eyeball.

K9 NARCAN 4mg Intranasal. Repeat every 3-5 mins.
HEAT STROKE Cool paws/ears. Temp >106 critical.
