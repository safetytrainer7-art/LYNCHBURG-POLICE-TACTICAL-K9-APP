K9 TACTICAL MEDIC & CPR

Cardiopulmonary Resuscitation (CPR)

Confirm absence of pulse. Position the K9 on their right side. For large breeds, perform compressions over the widest part of the chest at 100-120 BPM.

PROFESSIONAL VOICE: "Dispatch, ACSO K9 Unit 10-33. Initiating emergency field CPR on K9 asset. Clear the channel for priority medical traffic. Request Code-3 Veterinary response to my 20 immediately."

Hemorrhage & Tourniquet Application

Identify the source of arterial bleeding. High-and-tight application of the K9 tourniquet is required for extremity wounds.

PROFESSIONAL VOICE: "Officer Down! K9 Hemorrhage controlled with tourniquet application. Time of application: 1430 hours. Prepare for immediate medevac."
