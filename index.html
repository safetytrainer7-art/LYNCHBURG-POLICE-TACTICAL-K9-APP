Post-Apprehension Medical Care

The Duty: Officers must provide medical aid immediately after a K9 bite suspect is secured. Failure to treat can lead to "Deliberate Indifference" claims.

Application: Apply pressure to bite wounds immediately; summon EMS via radio as soon as handcuffs are secure.
