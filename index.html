K9 EMERGENCY FIRST AID

VOICE-ACTIVATED CPR

1. Lay dog on right side. 2. Hands over heart (elbow point). 3. Compress 2 inches deep.

BLOAT (GDV) PROTOCOL

SYMPTOMS: Sudden distended/hard abdomen, unsuccessful retching, excessive drooling, and pacing. This is a 10/10 time-sensitive emergency. The stomach has rotated, cutting off blood flow.

ACTION: Immediate transport to a surgical vet. Do not give any food/water. Keep the dog in a "chest-down" position if possible during transport.

NARCAN / OPIOID EXPOSURE

SYMPTOMS: Respiratory depression (slow breathing), pinpoint pupils, extreme lethargy, or collapse after a search.

ACTION: Administer 4mg Nasal Narcan immediately. If no improvement in 2-3 minutes, administer second dose. Be prepared for the dog to wake up disoriented or aggressive.

HEAT EXHAUSTION

SYMPTOMS: Heavy panting, dark red gums, temperature above 104°F. ACTION: Move to shade. Apply cool (not ice) water to paws, ears, and groin. Use a fan. Stop cooling once temp hits 103°F.

K9 LEGAL BIBLE

FEDERAL STANDARDS (4TH AMENDMENT)

RODRIGUEZ V. US: A police stop may not be prolonged for a K9 sniff without independent reasonable suspicion. The "mission" of the stop ends when tasks tied to the traffic infraction are or should have been completed.

FLORIDA V. HARRIS: If a dog is certified by a reputable agency or has recently completed a training program successfully, a court can rely on the dog's alert to establish probable cause.

GRAHAM V. CONNOR: K9 deployments are analyzed under the "objective reasonableness" standard. Consider the severity of the crime, the threat to officers, and whether the suspect is resisting/fleeing.

VIRGINIA SPECIFIC LAW

JARDINES/CURTILAGE: In Virginia, you cannot bring a dog onto the porch or curtilage of a home to sniff a door without a warrant or consent. A porch is a constitutionally protected area.

AUTOMOBILE EXCEPTION: Once a certified K9 gives a positive alert, Virginia law allows for a warrantless search of the entire vehicle and any containers that could contain the object of the search.

TACTICAL COMMAND GPS
NARCOTICS & SAFETY

FENTANYL / SYNTHETIC HAZMAT

IDENTIFICATION: Often found as "M30" blue pills, white powder, or multicolored "rainbow" blocks. 2mg is considered a lethal dose. NEVER field test suspected powders without a containment hood.

SAFETY: Wear Nitrile gloves. If powder is present, do not use air blowers or K9s in the immediate downwind area until cleared. Carry Narcan for yourself and your dog.

VIRGINIA DRUG WEIGHTS

METHAMPHETAMINE: 10 grams triggers mandatory minimums. COCAINE: 5 grams of base/crack triggers major penalties. Always document scales, packaging, and digital communication to support PWID (Possession with Intent to Distribute).
