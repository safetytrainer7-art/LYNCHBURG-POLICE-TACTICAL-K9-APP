K9 EMERGENCY SOS

FIELD TRAUMA PROTOCOLS

Immediate triage steps for field emergencies.

  • Heat Stress: Immediate shade. Cool water on foot pads/ears.
  • Trauma/Bleeding: Apply direct pressure with gauze or clean cloth.
  • Bloat: Observe for distended abdomen or dry heaving; transport immediately.
LEGAL SHIELD

GRAHAM v. CONNOR (1989)

The "Reasonableness" of a particular use of force must be judged from the perspective of a reasonable officer on the scene, rather than with the 20/20 vision of hindsight.
