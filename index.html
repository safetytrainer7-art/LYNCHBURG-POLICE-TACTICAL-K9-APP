K9 FIRST AID & TRAUMA

Gunshot & Penetrating Trauma

Assessment: Check for entry/exit wounds. Apply direct pressure to control hemorrhage. Use occlusive dressings for chest/abdominal wounds.

Snake Bites (Virginia Species)

Protocol: Identify the species if safe (Copperhead/Timber Rattler). Immobilize the affected limb at heart level. Do not apply ice or tourniquets. Transport immediately to a vet with antivenom.

NARCOTICS INTERDICTION

Vehicle Exterior Sniff

Strategy: Start down-wind. Observe K9 Change of Behavior (COB). A final response (sit/down) provides probable cause for a warrantless vehicle search under the Automobile Exception.
