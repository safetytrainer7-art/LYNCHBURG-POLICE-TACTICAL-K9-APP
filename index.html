K9 EMERGENCY FIELD MEDICINE

#1 K9 CPR: Place dog on right side. Heart behind left elbow. 100-120 bpm. 30 pumps, then 2 breaths into nose (hold mouth shut).
GUNSHOT/STAB: Pack wound with gauze. Constant pressure. Do not remove soaked pads.
HEAT EXHAUSTION: If temp >106, use water on paws/ears. Move to AC/Shade immediately.
SNAKE BITE: Calm dog. Immobilize limb. Transport to ER Vet.
