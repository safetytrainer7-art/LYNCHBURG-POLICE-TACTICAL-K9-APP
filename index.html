K9 FIRST AID

HEAT & BLOAT

Heatstroke: If temp >104°F, use cool water on belly/paws. Do NOT use ice water as it traps heat.

Bloat: Watch for retching and tight stomach. 10/10 emergency, transport immediately.

Narcan: 4mg nasal for opioid exposure. Use multiple doses for large dogs if needed.

LEGAL BIBLE

FEDERAL STANDARDS

Fourth Amendment: Sniffing a car in public is NOT a search (US v. Place). It only reveals contraband.

Rodriguez v. US: You cannot extend a traffic stop by 1 minute for a dog without independent suspicion.

Florida v. Harris: Certification by a reputable group = Probable Cause. Keep your training logs tight.

VIRGINIA SPECIFICS

Automobile Exception: A K9 alert gives you PC to search the whole vehicle immediately.

Curtilage: No dogs on porches without a warrant (Jardines). Stay in public walkways.

Testimony: Articulate the change in body posture and the final alert. VA judges focus on objective facts.

DRUGS & SAFETY

ID & HAZMAT

Fentanyl: Assume blue "M30" pills are lethal. Use Nitrile gloves. Never field test powders.

Exposure: Symptoms: pinpoint pupils/slow breathing. Use Narcan immediately. Notify dispatch for EMS.

VA Weights: 10g Meth or 5g Crack = mandatory minimums. Document scales and cash for PWID.
