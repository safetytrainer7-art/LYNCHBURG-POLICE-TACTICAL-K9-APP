K9 EMERGENCY FIRST AID

Field CPR Protocol

Position K9 on right side. Compress chest at 100-120 BPM. Use 30:2 compression-to-breath ratio through nostrils.

VOICE: "Dispatch, Unit 1. 10-33. Initiating field CPR. Request Priority Vet Transport to my 20."

Hemorrhage Control

Apply K9-specific tourniquet high and tight on the limb. For torso wounds, pack with hemostatic gauze and apply 5 minutes of pressure.

VOICE: "Officer Down! K9 tourniquet applied. Time: 1400 hours. Moving to extraction point."
