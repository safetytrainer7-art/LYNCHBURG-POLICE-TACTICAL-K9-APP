⚖️ FEDERAL & VIRGINIA LAW

Florida v. Harris: Training records = Reliability. If the dog is certified and training is current, a "miss" in the field doesn't destroy Probable Cause.

Rodriguez v. U.S.: You cannot extend a traffic stop specifically to wait for a K9. The sniff must happen within the "mission" of the original stop.

Virginia Case Law: An alert on a vehicle allows a search of the vehicle, but search of occupants requires independent articulable suspicion.

🚑 K9 EMERGENCY MEDICAL

BLOT (GDV): 10-43 Emergency. If the dog is dry-retching and the stomach is hard/swollen, transport to surgery immediately with sirens.

GSW/TRAUMA: Use a CAT tourniquet HIGH and TIGHT on limbs. For body wounds, pack tightly with gauze and apply pressure.

HEAT STROKE: Do not submerge in ice. Wet the paws and ears with cool water. Stop cooling when body temp hits 103.5°F.
